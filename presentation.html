The Problem

Customer support teams spend countless hours manually reading and triaging emails.

  • ⏳ Simple requests take time away from complex issues.
  • 😠 Angry customers wait in the same queue as routine inquiries.
  • 💸 High operational costs and slow resolution times.

Our Solution

A highly intelligent "first line of defense" combining AI reasoning with UiPath Maestro's enterprise governance.

  • 🧠 AI Agent: Reads incoming emails to extract structured JSON (intent, sentiment).
  • 🛤️ Maestro BPMN: Routes the ticket based on the AI's classification.
  • 🤖 RPA Automation: Instantly resolves simple queries (Happy Path).
  • 👨‍💻 Action Center: Escalates high-risk cases to human agents (Exception Path).

Technical Architecture

Built rapidly using Python and UiPath integrations.

  • LLM Engine: Prompt-engineered to output strict, deterministic JSON for system integration.
  • Routing Engine: BPMN Exclusive Gateways dynamically evaluate sentiment and intent variables.
  • Frontend UI: Custom glassmorphism web app to visualize the backend agentic routing in real-time.
